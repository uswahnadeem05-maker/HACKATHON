# Skills Demand Hub

AI-powered platform that analyzes Pakistan's job market and generates personalized learning roadmaps for students using Google Gemini AI.

Live Demo: https://hackathon-hxjgmz6bctg5wiwbq8t9bb.streamlit.app/

## Overview

Skills Demand Hub helps students discover what skills to learn based on real job market data. Students enter their current skills and target job role, and the app uses Google Gemini to generate a personalized learning roadmap with time estimates, free resources, and expected salary impact.

Developed for BuildByte Hackathon 2026 by Team Algogirls.

## Key Features

**Market Insights Dashboard** - Interactive visualization of top 10 in-demand skills, job distribution across Pakistani cities, and salary trends.

**AI Career Path Generator** - Students select current skills and target role. The app recommends 3 priority skills with learning timelines, free resources, and salary impact.

**Learning Resources Hub** - Curated free courses for each skill including YouTube, FreeCodeCamp, Khan Academy, and Kaggle Learn.

**Personal Progress Tracker** - Track skills learned, see visual progress bars, and unlock achievement milestones.

**Home Dashboard** - Metrics showing total jobs analyzed, unique skills in demand, and active job market cities.

## Technology Stack

Streamlit, Python, Pandas, Plotly, Google Gemini API, python-dotenv
